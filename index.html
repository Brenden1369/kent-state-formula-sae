About the Team

Kent State University Formula SAE is a student-run engineering organization dedicated to the design, manufacturing, and testing of a formula-style race car. The team provides hands-on STEM experience that prepares students for careers in engineering, manufacturing, and automation.

The Car

Each year, the team designs and builds a new vehicle to compete in the Formula SAE competition, focusing on performance, reliability, and innovation.
